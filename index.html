OUR`S SPECIAL

KITFO

Kitfo, a classic Ethiopian delicacy crafted with finely minced, tender beef infused with aromatic spices and Niter Kibbeh (Ethiopian spiced butter). Traditionally served with Ayib (Ethiopian cottage cheese) and a side of spicy Mitmita, it offers an authentic taste of Ethiopia that will leave you craving more. Experience the richness of Ethiopian culture today!

TIBS

Tibs, a traditional Ethiopian dish made with tender cuts of beef or lamb sautéed with onions, garlic, and a medley of spices. Served sizzling hot, it is often paired with Injera (Ethiopian flatbread) to create a meal full of bold and unforgettable flavors. Celebrate the essence of Ethiopian cuisine with every bite!

GENFO

Genfo, a traditional Ethiopian porridge made from barley or wheat flour, expertly prepared to a smooth and creamy texture. Topped with a pool of Niter Kibbeh (spiced butter) and seasoned with Berbere (Ethiopian chili powder), this comforting dish is perfect for breakfast or any time of the day. Immerse yourself in the warmth of Ethiopian culinary heritage today!

SHIRO

Quench your thirst with our Cold Drinks, a refreshing selection of beverages that offer a burst of cool, invigorating flavors. Whether you prefer classic sodas, sparkling waters, or fruity refreshments, each sip is a delightful way to stay cool and refreshed. Enjoy the chill!

DORO WOT

Doro Wot, Ethiopia's most cherished dish, made with tender chicken slow-cooked in a rich, spicy Berbere sauce. Enhanced with boiled eggs and flavored with Niter Kibbeh (spiced butter), this hearty stew is served with Injera (Ethiopian flatbread) for an authentic and unforgettable dining experience. Taste the heart of Ethiopian tradition today!

CHECHEBSA

Chechebsa, a traditional Ethiopian breakfast favorite made with shredded flatbread sautéed in spiced butter and flavored with Berbere (Ethiopian chili powder). Perfectly soft and bursting with flavor, it's a comforting dish often enjoyed with a cup of Ethiopian coffee or tea. Start your day the Ethiopian way!
